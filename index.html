Built Different Since 2013

We have continually made adjustments to our design to give you the strongest possible structure at the best price possible without compromising quality in areas that should not be compromised.

⚡

Fast Turnaround

Most shelters are built to order within 1–2 weeks, with delivery arranged as quickly as possible. Typical order-to-delivery time is 2–3 weeks when weather and ground conditions allow.

🛻

Truly Portable

Built on heavy-duty 6x6 pressure-treated skids with reinforced joinery to prevent racking during transport. The skid-mounted design allows you to easily move your barn with a truck or tractor whenever your needs change.

💪

Heavy-Duty Build

Built on real 6x6 and 4x6 lumber bases, with corners braced using double layers of pressure-treated 2x4s. The entire interior is lined with 5/8" plywood for an extremely sturdy and stiff structure.

Our Prices

Prices include free delivery up to 45 miles and a 5/8" plywood liner. Most styles come with a 2' overhang, which adds usable space beyond the listed size.

Sales tax applied at 9.75%. Prices subject to change. Custom sizes available upon request.

Delivery Policy

Free delivery for the first 45 miles (one way). Beyond that, a fee of $4 per mile applies for each additional mile.

Farm Tax Exemption

These shelters qualify as farm tax exempt if you have a current farm exemption ID in the buyer's name. A copy of your exemption (photo is OK) must be provided before we can confirm your tax-exempt total.
